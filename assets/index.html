$POM Miners

There are many advantages/disadvantages of Mining, but the majority of the holders suffered after effect of supply inflation. To overcome this, we will use Halving, Burn Mechanism for miners and Tax which will keep it Deflationary.

Burn - Burn is a utility for Miners to increase their cycle burn rate to get ahead in the race to mine more no. of blocks.This will reset every day to keep the burn going. This will make sure no disadvantage for the new miner.

Tax - Tax will be applicable for buy/sell tx. Initially, it will be used to add liquidity for the Exchanges.

Halving - Halving will be used for slashing the reward per block and for doubling the time for the burn mechanism.
